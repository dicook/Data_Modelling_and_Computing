Background

Federal elections are held in Australia approximately every three years, with the exact timing at the pleasure of the party in government. It is rumoured that the next election will be in May 2019. The ads have already started on TV, Australians arent going to cop it is the catch phrase of one minor party, robocalls have begun, and voting preference polling is in full swing. This assignment is asking you to work with different publicly available data on the election, with a goal to practice tidying and wrangling skills, with an added side effect of learning about the Australian political landscape.
